Rules


Welcome to our Ethereum Raffle!
Before participating, please read and understand the rules below:
STEPS

3.⁠ ⁠Enter the desired number of entries you want to purchase

RULES

(i.e. 100 entries), triggering the selection and payment of a verifiably random winner
